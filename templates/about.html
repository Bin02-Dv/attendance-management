about the software

Presently, the department of computer science Hassan usman polytechnic, Katsina is one of those departments that do not have a standard web C.A and Exam attendance tracking and management system. So it is very necessary to improve the way attendance is been carried out and managed. For instance, during a test or examination, sheets are provied to the students to register their names and registration number which will serve as an attendance sheet for the student who participated in the test, However, a student can invite his/her friend from within or outside the department who is good in a course to sit for him/her in a test or exam, which is a form of examination malpractice, and difficult for the lecturer to know. In other to avoid such, it is important to develope a well structured CA and Exam attendance tracking and management system for the department, which will eradicate the problems that occurs using manual attendance tracking and management system.
